# lamihti - Account Deletion Instructions

## App Name
lamihti (com.lamihti.lamihti_mobile)

## How to Delete Your Account

Account deletion is handled **inside the app**. Follow these steps:

1. Open the **lamihti** app
2. Navigate to the **Profile** screen
3. Tap on **Delete Account**
4. Confirm your choice

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

## Data Retention Policy

- All user data is deleted immediately upon account deletion
- No data is retained after deletion
- This action cannot be undone
